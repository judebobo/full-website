About Us

Ojay is a photographic artist fluent in the language of story telling. Creating beautiful imagery for over 15 years, for both couples and creatives in Nigeria and across the globe.
Ojay is best known for his honest and timeless photography style. With a unique talent for encompassing raw emotion in a frame and hypnotised by the romance of two souls in love, he captures moments unseen, effortlessly. Seamless in his execution, he leads with a sophistication of composition, stripping back the layers and capturing the real and the unfiltered.
